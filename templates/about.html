About the Movie Recommendation System

Project Overview

The Movie Recommendation System uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to analyze movie plot summaries and recommend films based on textual similarity. Unlike traditional recommendation systems that rely on ratings or watch history, our approach focuses on the actual content of movies to provide more relevant and personalized suggestions.

How It Works

Our system employs the following process to generate recommendations:

  1. Text Processing
    We process movie plot summaries by removing stop words, tokenizing, and vectorizing the text.
  2. TF-IDF Vectorization
    We use Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF) to convert text into numerical vectors.
  3. Similarity Calculation
    When you input your preferences, we calculate the cosine similarity between your input and all movie plots.
  4. Ranking
    Movies are ranked by similarity score, and the top matches are presented as recommendations.
Did you know? Cosine similarity measures the cosine of the angle between two vectors, providing a similarity score between -1 and 1, where 1 represents perfect similarity.
